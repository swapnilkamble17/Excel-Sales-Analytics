## 📌 Introduction
AtliQ Hardware manufactures and sells a diverse range of tech products such as personal computers, printers, and accessories. Their distribution network includes major retail partners like **Croma** and **Best Buy**, as well as online marketplaces such as **Flipkart** and **Amazon**. These channels help ensure wide accessibility and a strong customer reach.

## 🎯 Problem Statement
To design and develop interactive dashboards that deliver deep insights into AtliQ’s sales and financial performance. These dashboards help stakeholders make informed, data‑driven decisions and support strategic business planning.

---

## 📊 Sales Analysis

### **Objectives**
- **Customer Performance Report:** Analyze customer-wise sales trends across multiple years to identify top revenue contributors.
- **Division-Level Sales Report:** Compare sales across divisions for 2020 and 2021.
- **GM% By Quarters:** Evaluate quarterly gross margin percentages across sub-zones.
- **Market Performance vs Target:** Evaluate how markets performed against assigned targets.
- **New Products Performance:** Track sales generated by newly launched products.
- **Profit & Loss by Fiscal Year:** Summarize profit and loss statements across fiscal years.
- **Profit & Loss by Months:** Break down profit and loss data month-by-month.
- **Profit & Loss for Markets:** Analyze profit and loss performance across different markets.
- **Top 10 Products:** Highlight the top 10 products contributing to overall revenue.
- **Top 5 Countries:** Highlight highest revenue-generating countries.
- **Top & Bottom 5 Products:** Compare the best and worst performing products.

### **Key Insights**
- Revenue jumped **from $87.5M (2019) to $598.9M (2020)**.
- Majority of countries showed positive growth except *New Zealand, Norway, and Austria*.
- **P&A division** nearly tripled revenue (from $105.2M to $338.4M in 2021), while the **PC division** followed strongly.
- Leading new products: **AQ Qwerty ($22M)**, **AQ Trigger ($20.7M)**, **AQ Gen Y ($19.5M)**.
- **India** ranked highest in sales with **$161.3M**, followed by **USA ($87.8M)**.

### **Purpose of Sales Analytics**
To empower the business with actionable insights so teams can monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and optimize sales strategies.

### **Role of Reports**
- Identify customers eligible for discounts.
- Support negotiations using sales-backed evidence.
- Spot growth potential in emerging international markets.

## 💰 Finance Analysis

### **Objectives**
- **Yearly P&L:** Summarize annual financial outcomes.
- **Monthly P&L:** Detect monthly trends and patterns.
- **Market-wise P&L:** Provide granular financial insights across markets.
- **Quarterly GM% by Sub-Zone:** Assess profitability across different zones.

### **Key Insights**
- Net Sales grew by **204.5%** over three years.
- Profit margins stayed stable despite rising costs.
- Significant revenue expansion occurred in 2021.
- Profitability levels varied by region, supporting targeted strategy decisions.

### **Purpose of Finance Analytics**
To evaluate the company’s financial health, ensure informed planning, and support transparent communication with investors.

### **Role of Reports**
- Align financial insights with business strategy.
- Boost stakeholder and investor confidence.
